Our Story

Born From a Love
of Italian Food

Founded in 2008 by Chef Marco Bellini, Bella Vista was born from a simple dream — to share the heart of Italian cooking with New York. What started as a family trattoria has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Every dish tells a story. Every bite is a journey.
