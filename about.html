Who We Are

Our Story

Passion, tradition, and a pinch of Italian soul

Est. 2008

From Rome to New York,
With Love

Bella Vista was founded in 2008 by Chef Marco Bellini, a native of Rome who spent 20 years cooking in the finest trattorias of Italy before bringing his heritage to New York. His dream was simple: to recreate the warmth, flavour, and soul of an authentic Italian meal for every guest that walks through our door.

What began as a cozy 30-seat dining room on Italian Street has grown into one of Manhattan's most celebrated restaurants — yet our values remain unchanged. We import the finest ingredients directly from Italy: San Marzano tomatoes from Naples, aged Parmigiano Reggiano from Emilia-Romagna, extra-virgin olive oil from Tuscany, and Barolo wines from the Piedmont hills.

Every night at Bella Vista is a homecoming — for those who remember Italy, and a first discovery for those who are yet to fall in love with it.
